Welcome to Tetris

Instructions
  • Form as many rows as you can by moving or rotating the falling Tetris pieces.
    The more lines you make at once, the more score you get.
  • The game ends when you can no longer place another piece.
Movement & Rotation

Press arrow left, right, or down to move the pieces. Arrow up rotates them.

or
Save Piece

Press shift to save or swap the current piece in play.

You can do this once every time you get a new piece.
